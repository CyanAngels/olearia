Sound


The toolbar of the Olearia main window contains an item called sound this button will display the sound settings at the bottom of the main window. There are two items which can be set:


  1. Volume they playback volume of the book. You can also use your system volume as well.
  2. Speed. This option controls the rate at which the playback is played.


Both of these setting are stored on a per-book basis. This means that you can set these different for different books and Olearia will uses those settings when you continue reading that book.
