What is DAISY


DAISY digital talking books are the international standard for audio books for the blind and print disabled. They are offered in the following formats:


  • DAISY 2.02

    The original format used by many of the worlds libraries for the blind including Vision Australia and TPB in Sweden.

  • DAISY/NISO 2005

    Also called DAISY 3 or DAISY z3986 this is the newer format used by Beyond Books Beyond Barriers in Western Australia and Bookshare.org


In addition to the two format there are also three types of DAISY books in each version:


  • Full Text/Full Audio

    Both the text and the audio are present in the book. The current version of Olearia support the audio playback of this type of book. The final released version will also support the disply of the text as well as reading the text in the selected computer voice rather than the recorded audio

  • Audio Only

    Only the audio recording of the text is present. Olearia supports this type of book

  • Text Only

    Only the text is present in the book. There is no recorded audio. Bookshare.org books are an example of this type of book. The final release version of Olearia will support this type of book.
