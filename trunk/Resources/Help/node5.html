Opening a book


to open a book in Olearia you may use the Open item under the File menu or select command O. You wil then select on of the following types of files to open in the books directory:


  • .opf
  • .ncx
  • ncc.html


The first two types are found in DAISY/NISO 2005 books the ncc.html file is found in DAISY 2.02 books. It is also possible to open DAISY/NISO books by double clicking on the opf or ncx files.


Olearia keep a list of recent books in the Recent Books submenu of the File menu.

Playback


You can start and stop playback of a book by pressing the spacebar on you keyboard, selecting play or pause in the Navigation menu or by pressing the on screen Play/Pause button.
